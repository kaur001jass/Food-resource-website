About FRA

The Food Resource and Agriculture (FRA) is a specialized agency that leads international efforts to defeat hunger.

Our goal is to achieve food security for all and make sure that people have regular access to enough high-quality food to lead active, healthy lives.FRA works in over 130 countries worldwide.

Join us in creating a world without hunger and poverty.
